How to bulk upload images to your website.

You will need winSCP (http://winscp.sourceforge.net/eng/).

  1. Start WinSCP.
  2. Create a new connection or connect to your website name (e.g. wcoombs.jaraco.com)
  3. Log in using your username and password.
  4. A window will open to your home directory.  Your website is located in www/.  Your gallery is in www/gallery/.  You may upload files to www/gallery/upload/.
  5. To upload files, just drag-and-drop or cut/paste a source folder from your local computer to the upload folder in your gallery.
    Uploads should be in a separate folder under upload/.  If your images are not already grouped in a folder on your local system, you may create the folder on the web server and copy individual files to that folder.
  6. After files are uploaded, you may close WinSCP.
  7. Log into your gallery administration page (same as your gallery name with /admin appended).
  8. Edit the gallery to which you want to add photos or create a new gallery.
  9. From the Album Details page, click the link on the left: “Bulk Add Pictures”
  10. Chose one of the folders you created when uploading pictures.
  11. Chose one or more of the image files from that folder.  You can select multiple with Ctrl-click and select a range with Shift-click.
  12. Select any Upload Options you want.  I usually select “Set Title To File Name” and often select “Delete Directory after Adding”.
  13. Finally click “Add them!” and the images will be added to your album.  You may then edit the individual photos in the album to correct any titles or write any captions.
